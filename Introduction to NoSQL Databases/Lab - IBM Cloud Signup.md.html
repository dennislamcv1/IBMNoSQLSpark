Hands-on Lab: Sign Up for an IBM Cloud Account

Estimated time needed: 10 minutes

Objectives

In this lab you will:

  • Sign up for an IBM Cloud Account.

Sign up for an IBM Cloud Account

Step 1: Navigate to cloud.ibm.com

Step 2: Click on Create an account.

Step 3: Enter your email address.

Enter the password.

Make sure your password conforms to the security best practices that pop up.

Step 4: Click on the Next button.

Step 5: IBM emails you a verification code.

Check your email and key in the verification code.

Click Next.

Step 6: Enter your personal information.

Enter your first name.

Enter your last name.

Choose your Country or region.

Click Next.

Step 7: IBM displays the privacy notice.

Step 8: Once you have finished reading, scroll down and click on Proceed.

Step 9: You are logged in to IBM Cloud. You can now explore the IBM cloud offerings.
